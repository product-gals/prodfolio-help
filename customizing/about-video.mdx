A short video introduction can make your product manager portfolio more personal and memorable. It's a chance to show your personality beyond what a resume can convey, and hiring managers love it. 🎥

<Note>About video is a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

## Supported platforms

- **YouTube**
- **Loom**
- **Vimeo**
- **ScreenStudio**

## How to add a video

1. Open the portfolio builder
2. Go to the **Basics** section
3. Find the **About Video** field
4. Paste the URL from any supported platform
5. Optionally add a **video caption**
6. Optionally add a **thumbnail photo**

Prodfolio automatically detects the platform and generates the embed.

<Note>To use this feature, you must be on a Free Trial or the Pro plan.</Note>

## Tips

- Keep it under 5 minutes, viewers have short attention spans
- Introduce yourself, your product management background, and what you're looking for
- A casual, authentic tone works best. Just be you!
- Loom is great for quick, low-effort recordings
