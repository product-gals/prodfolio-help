A short video introduction can make your portfolio more personal and memorable.

## Supported platforms

- **YouTube**
- **Loom**
- **Vimeo**
- **ScreenStudio**

## How to add a video

1. Open the portfolio builder
2. Go to the **Basics** section
3. Find the **About Video** field
4. Paste the URL from any supported platform
5. Optionally add a **video caption**
6. Optionally add a **thumbnail URL**

Prodfolio automatically detects the platform and generates the embed.

## Tips

- Keep it under 2 minutes — viewers have short attention spans
- Introduce yourself, your background, and what you're looking for
- A casual, authentic tone works better than overly polished
- Loom is great for quick, low-effort recordings
