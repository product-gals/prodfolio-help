Prodfolio offers 5 preset themes to give your portfolio a distinct look and feel.

## Available themes

| Theme | Style |
|-------|-------|
| **Professional Default** | Clean and neutral — works for any industry |
| **Warm Welcome** | Friendly and approachable tones |
| **Professional Calm** | Cool, composed, and polished |
| **Modern Minimalist** | Simple and sleek with plenty of whitespace |
| **Earthy Warmth** | Warm, natural tones for a grounded feel |

## How to change your theme

1. Open the portfolio builder
2. Scroll to the **Theme** section
3. Click on any theme card to preview it
4. Your selection saves automatically
