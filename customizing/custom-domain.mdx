Connect your own domain (e.g., `portfolio.janedoe.com`) to your Prodfolio portfolio for a fully branded experience.

## Prerequisites

- A Pro plan subscription
- A domain you own with access to DNS settings

## Step-by-step setup

### 1. Enter your domain

1. Open the portfolio builder
2. Go to the **Domain** section
3. Enter your custom domain (e.g., `portfolio.janedoe.com`)
4. Click **Verify**

### 2. Configure DNS

Add a **CNAME record** in your domain registrar's DNS settings:

| Type | Name | Value |
|------|------|-------|
| CNAME | `portfolio` (or your subdomain) | Provided by Prodfolio |

<Note>DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate, though most complete within a few hours.</Note>

### 3. Verify

Return to Prodfolio and click **Verify** again. Once DNS has propagated, your domain status will change to **Active**.

## Domain status

- **Pending** — DNS not yet verified
- **Active** — Domain is connected and working
- **Error** — Something went wrong (check your DNS settings)

## Troubleshooting

- **Domain still showing "Pending"?** DNS propagation can take up to 48 hours. Try again later.
- **Error status?** Double-check that your CNAME record matches exactly what Prodfolio provided.
- **Using a root domain (no subdomain)?** Some registrars require an ALIAS or ANAME record instead of CNAME for root domains.

## Known limitations

<Warning>Inline editing of section titles and subtitles is not available on custom domains. Your Prodfolio login session doesn't carry over to external domains, so the edit controls won't appear.</Warning>

**Workaround:** Edit your titles and subtitles on your Prodfolio-hosted portfolio before setting up your custom domain — changes carry over automatically. Or contact **support@prodfolio.io** and we'll update them for you.
