Every portfolio section has a **title** (e.g., "Case Studies") and a **subtitle** (e.g., "How I've solved product challenges"). You can edit both directly on your published portfolio to better reflect your role and product management experience. Make your PM portfolio feel like *yours*. ✏️

## Default section titles and subtitles

| Section | Default Title | Default Subtitle |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| Work Experience | Professional Timeline | My product management journey |
| Case Studies | Case Studies | How I've solved product challenges |
| References | References | What colleagues and collaborators say about working with me |
| Philosophy | My Product Philosophy | How I think about building products |
| Beyond Work | Beyond Work | Projects and contributions outside of my day job |

## How to edit

1. Visit your **published portfolio** while logged in (not the builder)
2. Hover over any section title or subtitle — a pencil icon appears
3. Click the pencil icon to enter edit mode
4. Type your new text
5. Click away (or press **Enter**) to save — you'll see a brief "Saved" confirmation

**To cancel:** Press **Escape** while editing to revert to the previous value.

**To reset to default:** Clear the text and click away. The default title or subtitle will be restored.

## When to customize

- Your role is different from a traditional product manager (e.g., program manager, project manager, product ops)
- You want to tailor the language to match a specific role you're targeting
- You want your portfolio to feel more personal and less templated

## Examples

| Default | Customized |
|---------|-----------|
| Case Studies | Product Launches |
| My Product Philosophy | How I Think About Program Management |
| My product management journey | My path to product leadership |
| How I've solved product challenges | Cross-functional programs I've led |

## Custom domain limitation

<Warning>Inline editing of titles and subtitles is not currently available when viewing your portfolio on a custom domain. This is because the editing session is tied to your Prodfolio login, which doesn't carry over to external domains.</Warning>

**Workaround:** Contact us at **support@prodfolio.io** with the titles and subtitles you'd like, and we'll update them for you.

You can also edit your titles and subtitles *before* setting up a custom domain — the changes will carry over once the domain is active.
