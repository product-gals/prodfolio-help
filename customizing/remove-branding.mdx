sidebarTitle: Remove Branding (Pro)

By default, your public portfolio includes a small "Built with Prodfolio" badge. Pro users can remove this for a fully clean, branded product manager portfolio.

<Note>Removing branding is a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

## How to remove it

This option is available in your portfolio settings. Once you upgrade, the badge is automatically hidden from your public portfolio. ✨

<Tip>Even without the badge, your portfolio URL will still include `app.prodfolio.io` unless you set up a [custom domain](/customizing/custom-domain). Both are included on the Pro plan.</Tip>
