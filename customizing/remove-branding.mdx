<Note>Removing branding is a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

By default, your public portfolio includes a small "Built with Prodfolio" badge. Pro users can remove this for a fully clean, branded product manager portfolio.

## How to remove it

This option is available in your portfolio settings. Once enabled, the badge is hidden from your public portfolio. Clean and professional — just like your PM work. ✨

<Tip>Even without the badge, your portfolio URL will still include `prodfolio.io` unless you set up a [custom domain](/customizing/custom-domain).</Tip>
