Want your product manager portfolio to match your personal brand down to the pixel? Pro users can fully customize their portfolio's color palette using hex codes. 🎨

<Note>Custom colors are a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

## Customizable colors

- **Primary**: Main accent color (buttons, links)
- **Secondary**: Supporting color
- **Accent**: Highlight color for emphasis
- **Background**: Page background color
- **Text**: Body text color

## How to set custom colors

1. Open the portfolio builder
2. Scroll to the **Theme** section
3. Select **Custom** to get started
4. Click on a color field and enter a hex code (`#7C3AED`)
5. Click **Update** in the upper right hand side of the builder to save your changes

<Note>To use this feature, you must be on a Free Trial or the Pro plan.</Note>

## Contrast ratios

Prodfolio checks the contrast ratio between your text and background colors to help ensure readability. If contrast is too low, you'll see a warning, because a beautiful portfolio should also be an accessible one.

## Resetting to a preset

Click **Reset to Preset** to revert all custom colors back to the selected theme's defaults.
