When approving an access request for your portfolio, you choose how long the visitor can view the case study. Flexibility is key here. 🔑

## Duration options

| Duration | Best for |
|----------|----------|
| **1 hour** | Quick review during a call or interview |
| **24 hours** | Giving someone a day to review at their pace |
| **7 days** | Extended review period for hiring teams |
| **Indefinite** | Trusted contacts who should always have access |

## How it works

- Access starts when you approve the request
- The countdown begins immediately
- When the duration expires, the visitor loses access automatically
- You can [revoke access](/access/revoking-access) before the duration expires
