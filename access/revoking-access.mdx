---
title: Revoking Access
description: Revoke access to password-protected case studies on your PM portfolio at any time. Stay in control of who sees your work.
---

Changed your mind? You can revoke access to a password-protected case study at any time, even before the grant duration expires.

## How to revoke

1. Go to **Access Management** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Active** tab
3. Find the grant you want to revoke
4. Click **Revoke**

The visitor will immediately lose access to the case study.
