---
title: Revoking Access
---

Changed your mind? You can revoke access to a locked case study at any time, even before the grant duration expires.

## How to revoke

1. Go to **Settings** → **Access** tab
2. Click the **Active** tab
3. Find the grant you want to revoke
4. Click **Revoke**

The visitor will immediately lose access to the case study, but they will not be notified that you've revoked their access.
