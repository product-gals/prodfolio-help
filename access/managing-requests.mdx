---
title: Managing Access Requests
description: How to approve, reject, and manage case study access requests.
---

<Note>Access management is a Pro feature tied to [password-protected case studies](/publishing/password-protection).</Note>

When visitors request access to a password-protected case study, their requests appear in your Access Management dashboard.

## Viewing requests

Go to **Access Management** in the sidebar. The **Pending** tab shows all unreviewed requests with:
- Which portfolio and case study they're requesting
- The requester's email address
- When they submitted the request

## Approving a request

1. Click **Approve** on a pending request
2. Choose an access duration:
   - 1 hour
   - 24 hours
   - 7 days
   - Indefinite
3. An access link is generated for the requester

## Rejecting a request

Click **Reject** to deny the request. The requester will not be notified (they simply won't receive access).
