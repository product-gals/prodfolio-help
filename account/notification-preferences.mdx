## Quarterly update reminders

<Note>Quarterly reminders are a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

Pro users can opt in to receive quarterly email reminders to update their product manager portfolio. Keeping your portfolio current ensures it always reflects your latest work — because a stale portfolio is a missed opportunity. 📅

Toggle this on or off in **Settings** → **Preferences**.

## Other notifications

Prodfolio sends transactional emails for:
- Email verification
- Password reset
- Access requests (for password-protected case studies)
