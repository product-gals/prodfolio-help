Manage email notifications and quarterly portfolio update reminders on your Prodfolio account.

## Quarterly update reminders

Pro users can opt in to receive quarterly email reminders to update their product manager portfolio. Keeping your portfolio current ensures it always reflects your latest work, because a stale portfolio is a missed opportunity. 📅

Toggle this on or off in **Settings** → **Preferences**.

<Note>Quarterly reminders are a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

## Product updates

If you opted in during sign-up, you'll receive occasional emails about new features, promotions, and PM career tips. You can unsubscribe from these at any time.

## Required notifications

Some emails are sent to all users regardless of preferences:
- **Terms of Service and Privacy Policy updates**: We'll notify you whenever there are changes to our legal policies
- **Email verification**
- **Password reset**
- **Access requests** (for locked case studies)
