## Quarterly update reminders

<Note>Quarterly reminders are a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

Pro users can opt in to receive quarterly email reminders to update their portfolio. Keeping your portfolio current ensures it always reflects your latest work.

Toggle this on or off in **Settings** → **Preferences**.

## Other notifications

Prodfolio sends transactional emails for:
- Email verification
- Password reset
- Access requests (for password-protected case studies)
