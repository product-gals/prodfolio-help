# Editing Your Profile

Your profile settings apply across all your portfolios.

## How to edit

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile** tab
2. Update any of the following:
   - **Full name**
   - **Professional title**
   - **LinkedIn URL**
   - **Email address**
3. Click **Save Changes**

## Profile photo

Profile photo upload is coming soon. In the meantime, your initials are displayed as an avatar.
