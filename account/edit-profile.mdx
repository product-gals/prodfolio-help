---
title: Editing Your Profile
description: Update your name, professional title, LinkedIn, and email on your Prodfolio account. Changes apply across all portfolios.
---

Your profile settings apply across all your product manager portfolios.

## How to edit

1. Go to **Settings** → **Profile** tab
2. Update any of the following:
   - **Full name**
   - **Professional title**
   - **LinkedIn URL**
   - **Email address**
3. Click **Save Changes**

## Profile photo

You can upload a headshot in the **Basics** section of the portfolio builder. Supported formats are JPEG, PNG, and WebP (max 5 MB). Your photo displays on your public portfolio. If no photo is uploaded, your initials are shown as an avatar.
