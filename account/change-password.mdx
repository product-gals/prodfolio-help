---
title: Changing Your Password
---

Change your Prodfolio account password or manage Google OAuth login. Password requirements and reset instructions.

## Email/password users

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account** tab
2. Enter your new password and confirm it
3. Click **Save**

### Password requirements

- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character

## Google OAuth users

If you signed up with Google, you don't have a Prodfolio password. Your authentication is managed through your Google account. To change your login credentials, update your Google account password directly.

## Forgot your password?

Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to receive a password reset email.
