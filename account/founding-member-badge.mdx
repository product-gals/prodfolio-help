# Founding Member Badge

If you subscribed to Pro at the founding member rate, you have access to a special badge on your portfolio.

## What it is

A small crown icon that appears on your public portfolio, indicating you're a Prodfolio founding member.

## Showing or hiding it

1. Go to **Settings** → **Preferences**
2. Toggle **Founding Member Badge** on or off

## What are founding member perks?

As a founding member, you get:
- **Locked-in pricing** — Your rate never increases, even as standard pricing goes up
- **Private Slack community** — Connect with other founding members
- **AMAs with founders** — Direct access to the Prodfolio team
- **Portfolio review** — A personal portfolio review within 30 days of subscribing
- **First access to new features** — Be the first to try what's new
