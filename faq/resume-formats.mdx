---
title: What file formats does resume upload support?
---

Upload your product manager resume in PDF or Word format. Learn which file types work best with AI resume parsing.

Prodfolio supports:
- **PDF** (.pdf)
- **Word** (.doc, .docx)

Max file size: 5MB

For best results with AI parsing, use a cleanly formatted product manager resume without complex tables or multi-column layouts. Simple and clean wins the race here!
