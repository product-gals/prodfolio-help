# How do I contact support?

## Email

Reach us at **support@prodfolio.io** for any questions, issues, or feedback.

## In-app feedback

Use the feedback widget inside the Prodfolio app to submit feature requests, report bugs, or share suggestions. Look for the feedback button in the bottom-right corner of the app.

## Response time

We're a small team and typically respond within 24-48 hours on business days.
