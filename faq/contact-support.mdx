---
title: How do I contact support?
---

Get help from the Prodfolio team. Contact us via email or in-app feedback for questions about your product manager portfolio.

## Email

Reach us at **support@prodfolio.io** for any questions, issues, or feedback. We're real humans (and PMs ourselves!) who actually read every message. 💜

## In-app feedback

Use the feedback widget inside the Prodfolio app to submit feature requests, report bugs, or share suggestions. Look for the feedback button in the bottom-right corner of the app.

## Submit feedback

- [Submit a bug report](https://tally.so/r/1AAPgg)
- [Submit a feature request](https://tally.so/r/VL0M1a)
- [View our product roadmap](https://prodfolio-roadmap.notion.site/?v=30cf710757778193ab23000cf8ad4811&pvs=74)

## Response time

We're a small team and typically respond within 24-48 hours on business days. We're building Prodfolio for PMs - your feedback shapes what we build next.
