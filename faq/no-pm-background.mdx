---
title: Can I use Prodfolio without a PM background?
---

Absolutely! 💯 Prodfolio is designed for product managers at all stages, including people who are **aspiring to break into product management**.

During onboarding, you can select "Aspiring PM" as your career stage. The platform works just as well for showcasing transferable experience, side projects, and relevant case studies from adjacent roles. We see tons of aspiring PMs using Prodfolio to tell their story.

The SIGNAL framework for case studies can be applied to any project where you identified a problem, gathered insights, set goals, navigated decisions, achieved results, and learned from the experience - regardless of your current title. You don't need "Product Manager" on your business card to build a killer portfolio.

<Tip>We've also seen success with users in Program Management, UX Design, and Product Marketing!</Tip>
