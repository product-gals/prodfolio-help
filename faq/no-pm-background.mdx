---
title: Can I use Prodfolio without a PM background?
description: Whether Prodfolio works for aspiring product managers.
---

Yes! Prodfolio is designed for product managers at all stages, including people who are **aspiring to break into PM**.

During onboarding, you can select "Aspiring PM" as your career stage. The platform works just as well for showcasing transferable experience, side projects, and relevant case studies from adjacent roles.

The SIGNAL framework for case studies can be applied to any project where you identified a problem, gathered insights, set goals, navigated decisions, achieved results, and learned from the experience — regardless of your current title.
