---
title: Can I have multiple portfolios?
description: Create multiple product manager portfolios with Prodfolio Pro. Free plan includes 1 portfolio, Pro gives you unlimited.
---

- **Free plan:** 1 product manager portfolio
- **Pro plan:** Unlimited portfolios

This is great if you want to tailor different PM portfolios for different roles or industries — for example, one for B2B product management and another for consumer products. 🎯

If you downgrade from Pro to Free, your additional portfolios are preserved but become inaccessible until you upgrade again.
