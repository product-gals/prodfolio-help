---
title: How do I delete my account?
---

To delete your Prodfolio account, contact us at **support@prodfolio.io** with your request.

## What happens when you delete your account

- All your portfolios and case studies are permanently deleted
- Your profile data is removed
- If you have an active subscription, it will be canceled
- Your portfolio URL becomes available for others

<Warning>Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to export or save any content you want to keep before requesting deletion.</Warning>
