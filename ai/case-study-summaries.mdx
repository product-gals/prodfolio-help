Hiring managers are busy — they might not read every word of every case study. Prodfolio can automatically generate concise summaries of your product case studies, making them easier for visitors to scan before diving into the full writeup. 📋

## How it works

- Summaries are generated automatically from your case study content
- They appear on your portfolio's case study cards, giving visitors a quick preview
- Free users see a blurred preview of the summary

## What makes a good summary

The AI pulls from your SIGNAL sections to create a summary that highlights:
- The product problem you solved
- Your approach
- The key results

For the best summaries, make sure your case study sections are detailed and specific.
