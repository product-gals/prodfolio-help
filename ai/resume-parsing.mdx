# AI Resume Parsing

Upload your resume and let Prodfolio's AI extract your information to automatically fill in your portfolio.

## What it extracts

- **Basics** — Name, professional title, contact information
- **About** — Professional summary
- **Experience** — Companies, titles, dates, and role descriptions

## How to use it

1. Go to the **Resume** section in the portfolio builder
2. Upload a PDF or DOCX file
3. The AI parses your resume and shows you what it found
4. Select which sections you want to auto-populate
5. Review and adjust the imported data

## Availability

Resume parsing is **free and unlimited** on all plans.

## Tips

- Use a cleanly formatted resume for best results
- Review all parsed data — AI may occasionally misinterpret formatting
- You can re-upload a new resume at any time
- Importing data won't overwrite sections you've already filled in without your confirmation
