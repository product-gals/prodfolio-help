title: AI Case Study Generation
---

Prodfolio uses AI to help you draft product case study content, so you can focus on refining rather than staring at a blank page. Think of it as your PM writing assistant. ✨

## How it works

1. Head to the builder and click **Add Case Study**
2. Use the AI generation option to create draft content for any SIGNAL section
3. Review and edit the generated content to match your voice and add details the AI couldn't know

<Tip>Missing artifacts? We've all been there. If you have been using something like Claude or ChatGPT to work through your work experience, try exporting some of your work content from your trusted AI source.</Tip>

## Credit limits

| Plan | AI Generation Credits |
|------|----------------------|
| Free | 3 case study generations |
| Pro | Unlimited |

<Note>Once you've used your 3 free credits, you'll need to upgrade to Pro for unlimited AI case study generations.</Note>

## Tips

- AI-generated content is a starting point, not a finished product, always personalize it with your product management perspective
- The more context you provide in earlier sections, the better the AI can generate later sections
- Specific metrics and outcomes should always be added manually for accuracy
