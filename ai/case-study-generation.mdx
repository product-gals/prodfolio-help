Prodfolio uses AI to help you draft case study content, so you can focus on refining rather than writing from scratch.

## How it works

1. Start a new case study or open an existing one
2. Use the AI generation option to create draft content for any SIGNAL section
3. Review and edit the generated content to match your voice and add details the AI couldn't know

## Credit limits

| Plan | AI Generation Credits |
|------|----------------------|
| Free | 3 case study generations |
| Pro | Unlimited |

<Note>Once you've used your 3 free credits, you'll need to upgrade to Pro for additional AI generations.</Note>

## Tips

- AI-generated content is a starting point, not a finished product — always personalize it
- The more context you provide in earlier sections, the better the AI can generate later sections
- Specific metrics and outcomes should always be added manually for accuracy
