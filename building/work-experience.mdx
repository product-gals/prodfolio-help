# Adding Work Experience

Showcase your career history with up to 10 work experience entries.

## Adding a role

For each entry, provide:
- **Company name**
- **Job title**
- **Start date** (month and year)
- **End date** (month and year, or check "Current" for your present role)

## Adding descriptions

Toggle **Show descriptions** to add detail about each role. Use this to highlight key accomplishments, team size, or scope of impact.

## Customizing the section title

On your published portfolio, this section is titled **"Professional Timeline"** by default. You can rename it inline — hover over the title while viewing your live portfolio to edit.

[Learn more about customizing section titles →](/customizing/section-titles-subtitles)

## Tips

- List roles in reverse chronological order (most recent first)
- Focus on impact and outcomes, not just responsibilities
- Keep descriptions concise — your case studies are where you go deep
- You can add up to 10 entries
