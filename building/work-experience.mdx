title: Adding Work Experience
description: Add your product management work experience to your portfolio — roles, companies, dates, and descriptions.
---

Your work experience section is where you show the trajectory of your product management career. Showcase up to 10 roles to give visitors a clear picture of where you've been and what you've built. 📈

## Adding a role

For each entry, provide:
- **Company name**
- **Job title** (e.g., "Product Manager," "Senior PM," "Director of Product")
- **Start date** (month and year)
- **End date** (month and year, or check "Current" for your present role)

## Adding descriptions

Toggle **Show descriptions** to add detail about each role. Use this to highlight key accomplishments, team size, or scope of impact. Keep it punchy — your case studies are where you go deep on the stories behind the work.

## Customizing the section title

On your published portfolio, this section is titled **"Professional Timeline"** by default. You can rename it inline — hover over the title while viewing your live portfolio to edit.

[Learn more about customizing section titles →](/customizing/section-titles-subtitles)

## Tips

- List roles in reverse chronological order (most recent first)
- Focus on impact and outcomes, not just responsibilities
- Keep descriptions concise — save the deep dives for your product case studies
- You can add up to 10 entries
