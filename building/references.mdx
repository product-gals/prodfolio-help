---
title: Adding References
---

References add serious credibility to your product manager portfolio by showcasing what others say about working with you. Think of them as built-in testimonials & social proof that you're as great to work with as your case studies suggest. 🤝

## Adding a reference

For each reference (up to 6), provide:
- **Name**: The person's full name
- **Title**: Their professional title
- **Relationship**: How you worked together (Manager, Colleague, Client, Mentor, Direct Report, Other)
- **Quote**: What they said about you (up to 1,500 characters)

## Featuring a reference

Click the star icon to **feature** a reference. Featured references are highlighted more prominently on your public portfolio.

## Tips

- Always ask for permission before adding someone as a reference
- Quotes that mention specific product outcomes or PM skills are most impactful
- A mix of relationship types (manager + colleague + client) shows breadth
- You can drag to reorder references

## Customizing the section title

On your published portfolio, this section is titled **"References"** by default. You can rename it inline by hovering over the title while viewing your live portfolio to edit.
