# Writing Your About Section

The About section tells visitors who you are as a product professional.

## Professional summary

A concise overview of your background and what you bring to the table. Think of it as an expanded elevator pitch.

## Expertise areas

Select from predefined areas that match your experience:

- Platform PM, Growth PM, AI/ML PM, Technical PM, Data PM
- Consumer PM, B2B PM, Subscriptions/DTC, Payments

You can also add **custom expertise tags** for areas not in the list.

## Product philosophy

A longer-form section (up to 2,000 characters) where you can share your approach to product management. We recommend 300–500 words covering:

- How you think about building products
- What principles guide your decisions
- What makes you unique as a PM

<Tip>Your product philosophy is what differentiates you from other PMs with similar titles. Use it to show how you think, not just what you've done.</Tip>

## Customizing section titles

On your published portfolio, the philosophy section is titled **"My Product Philosophy"** by default. You can rename it inline — hover over the title while viewing your live portfolio to edit.
