# Beyond Work Links

The Beyond Work section lets you showcase what you do outside of your day job — side projects, writing, podcasts, community involvement, and more.

## Adding a link

For each link (up to 8), provide:
- **Type** — Blog, Podcast, Side Project, Community, Newsletter, or Other
- **URL** — The link to your content
- **Title** — A display name for the link
- **Description** — A short summary of what it is
- **CTA text** — The button label (auto-suggested based on type, e.g., "Read Blog" or "Listen Now")

## Tips

- This section shows you're a well-rounded PM with interests beyond the job
- Side projects and writing samples demonstrate initiative and communication skills
- You can drag to reorder links

## Customizing the section title

On your published portfolio, this section is titled **"Beyond Work"** by default. You can rename it inline — hover over the title while viewing your live portfolio to edit.
