# Writing Case Studies with SIGNAL

Case studies are the most impactful part of your Prodfolio portfolio. They show *how* you work, not just where you've worked. Prodfolio uses the **SIGNAL framework** to guide you through writing them.

## The SIGNAL Framework

Each case study follows six steps:

### 1. Situation
What was the problem or opportunity? Set the scene — describe the context, the team, the product, and why this work mattered.

### 2. Insight
What data or research informed your approach? Share user research findings, metrics, competitive analysis, or stakeholder feedback that shaped your thinking.

### 3. Goals
What were the product outcomes you were aiming for? Define the success metrics and objectives you set.

### 4. Navigation
How did you approach the problem? Describe your strategy, the tradeoffs you considered, and how you made decisions.

### 5. Achievement
What were the results? Share quantitative and qualitative outcomes. Numbers matter here.

### 6. Learnings
What did you learn? Reflect on what worked, what didn't, and what you'd do differently.

## Creating a case study

1. Go to the **Case Studies** section in the portfolio builder
2. Click **Add Case Study**
3. Fill in the overview: title, slug, TL;DR summary, industry, and your role
4. Set the **context type**: Day Job, Internship, Side Project, Coursework, or Volunteer
5. Set the **status**: Complete or Active (for work-in-progress projects)
6. Work through each SIGNAL step using the stepper or overview mode
7. Add media to any section for visual context

## Case study overview fields

Beyond the SIGNAL sections, each case study has metadata that helps visitors understand the context at a glance:

- **Title** — The name of your case study
- **TL;DR** — A short summary (up to 200 characters) that appears on case study cards
- **Context type** — Whether this was a Day Job, Internship, Side Project, Coursework, or Volunteer project
- **Status** — Mark as Complete or Active (for ongoing work)
- **Industry** — The industry the work relates to
- **Company** — The company or organization involved
- **Skills & tools** — Tags relevant to this specific case study
- **Timeline / Role** — Your role and the timeframe

## Featuring a case study

Click the **star icon** on any case study to mark it as **Featured**. Featured case studies are highlighted more prominently on your public portfolio. This is available on all plans.

## Adding media to SIGNAL sections

Each SIGNAL section supports media attachments to add visual context:

- **Images** — Up to 3 per section (PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP, GIF — max 5 MB each)
- **Videos** — 1 per section, embedded via URL from YouTube, Loom, Vimeo, or ScreenStudio

You can add optional captions to both images and videos.

## Section word limits

Each SIGNAL section has a recommended word limit to keep your writing focused:

| Section | Word limit |
|---------|-----------|
| Situation | 75 words |
| Insight | 140 words |
| Goals | 120 words |
| Navigation | 120 words |
| Achievement | 120 words |
| Learnings | 120 words |

The editor shows a word count indicator as you write.

## Limits

- Free and Pro plans: up to **6 case studies** per portfolio

<Tip>Start with your strongest case study. Quality beats quantity — one well-written case study is more impressive than three rushed ones.</Tip>

## Customizing the section title

On your published portfolio, this section is titled **"Case Studies"** by default. You can rename it inline — hover over the title while viewing your live portfolio to edit. For example, you might change it to "Product Launches" or "Program Implementations."
