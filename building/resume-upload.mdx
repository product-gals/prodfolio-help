Got a resume ready? Prodfolio can parse your product manager resume with AI to automatically fill in your portfolio sections — saving you a ton of time on data entry. Upload it, review the results, and you're halfway to a finished PM portfolio. ⚡

## Supported formats

- **PDF** (.pdf)
- **Word** (.docx)

## How it works

1. Go to the **Resume** section in the portfolio builder
2. Click **Upload Resume** and select your file
3. Prodfolio's AI parses the content and extracts structured data from your resume
4. A modal appears showing what was found — select which portfolio sections to auto-populate:
   - **Basics** (name, title, contact info)
   - **About** (professional summary)
   - **Experience** (work history with companies, titles, dates)

## Credits

Resume parsing is **unlimited on all plans** — both Free and Pro users can parse as many resumes as they need. No limits here. 🎉

<Note>AI parsing works best with clearly formatted resumes. If your resume has unusual formatting, some fields may need manual adjustment.</Note>

## Managing your resume

- Your uploaded resume filename and upload date are displayed in the builder
- You can delete your resume and upload a new one at any time
- The parsed data becomes part of your portfolio — deleting the resume doesn't remove the data
