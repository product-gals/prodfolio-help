The Basics section is the foundation of your portfolio — it's the first thing visitors see.

## What to fill in

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| First name | Yes | Displays prominently at the top |
| Last name | Yes | |
| Professional title | Yes | e.g., "Senior Product Manager at Acme" |
| Location | No | City, state, or "Remote" |
| LinkedIn URL | No | Links to your LinkedIn profile |
| GitHub URL | No | Links to your GitHub profile |
| Contact email | No | Allows visitors to reach you |

## Availability status

Let recruiters and hiring managers know if you're open to new opportunities:

- **Available** — Actively looking
- **Open to Opportunities** — Not actively searching but open to the right role
- **Not Looking** — Not available right now

When set to Available or Open, you can specify what you're open to:
- Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Consulting, Advisory
- Remote, Hybrid, On-site

## Headshot

Upload a profile photo to display on your portfolio. Supported formats are JPEG, PNG, and WebP (max 5 MB). You can remove or replace your photo anytime.

Headshot upload is available on all plans.
