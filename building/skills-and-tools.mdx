These sections let you quickly communicate your competencies and technical proficiency.

## Skills

Add tags for your product management skills — e.g., "User Research," "A/B Testing," "Roadmapping," "Stakeholder Management."

## Tools

Add tags for the tools and technologies you use — e.g., "Jira," "Figma," "Amplitude," "SQL," "Mixpanel."

## Tips

- Be specific rather than generic ("A/B Testing" is better than "Data Analysis")
- Include tools relevant to the roles you're targeting
- Tags are displayed as badges on your public portfolio
