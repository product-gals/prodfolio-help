Your skills and tools sections help recruiters and hiring managers quickly see what you bring to the table as a product manager. Think of them as the "at a glance" view of your PM toolkit. 🧰

## Skills

Add tags for your product management skills in each case study: "User Research," "A/B Testing," "Roadmapping," "Stakeholder Management," "Go-to-Market Strategy."

## Tools

Add tags for the tools and technologies you use: "Jira," "Figma," "Amplitude," "SQL," "Mixpanel." Hiring managers often search for PMs with specific tool experience, so be thorough here.

## Tips

- Be specific rather than generic ("A/B Testing" is better than "Data Analysis")
- Include tools relevant to the product management roles you're targeting
- Tags are displayed as badges on your public portfolio
