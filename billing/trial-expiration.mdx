# What Happens When My Trial Expires?

If your 14-day trial ends and you haven't subscribed to Pro, your account automatically reverts to the Free plan.

## What you keep

- All your portfolio data, case studies, and content
- Your first portfolio (published or draft)
- Basic analytics (view count)
- References section
- Unlimited resume parsing

## What gets locked

- Additional portfolios (if you created more than one)
- Custom hex color themes (reverts to preset)
- Custom domain
- Password-protected case studies
- About video
- Advanced analytics (traffic sources, case study rankings)
- Unlimited AI case study generation (reverts to 3 credits)
- AI case study summaries
- Remove branding option
- Quarterly update reminders

## Upgrading after trial expiration

You can upgrade to Pro at any time to restore access to all features. Your data is never deleted.
