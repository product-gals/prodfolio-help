Every Pro subscription starts with a 14-day free trial so you can explore all Pro features for your product manager portfolio before committing. No risk, no pressure. 🙌

## How to start

1. Click **Upgrade to Pro** from any upgrade prompt in the app, or go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Choose monthly or annual billing
3. Complete checkout through Stripe
4. Your 14-day trial begins immediately

## During your trial

- You have full access to all Pro features
- A trial countdown is visible in your billing settings
- You can switch between monthly and annual billing during the trial
- You can cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid being charged

## What happens when the trial ends

- **If you keep your subscription:** You'll be charged at your selected billing interval
- **If you cancel before the trial ends:** You won't be charged, and your account reverts to the Free plan
