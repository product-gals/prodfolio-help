---
title: The Founding Member Offer
---

Prodfolio's Founding Member offer gives early adopters a permanently discounted Pro plan with exclusive perks. It's our way of saying thanks to the product managers who believed in us early. 💜

## Pricing

| Billing | Founding Member | Standard (future) |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Monthly | `$15/mo` | `$29/mo` |
| Annual | `$129/yr` (~`$10.75/mo`) | `$278/yr` (~`$23.17/mo`) |

**Your founding member rate is locked forever.** Even as standard pricing increases, your rate stays the same.

## Founding Member perks

- **Locked-in pricing**: Your rate never increases, even as standard pricing goes up
- **Private Slack community**: Connect with other founding members
- **AMAs with founders**: Direct access to the Prodfolio team
- **Portfolio review**: A personal portfolio review within 30 days of subscribing
- **First access to new features**: Be the first to try what's new
- **Founding Member badge**: An optional crown icon on your public portfolio

## 14-day free trial

Every Pro subscription starts with a 14-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime.
