# Managing Your Subscription

## Billing portal

Click **Manage Subscription** in **Settings** → **Billing** to open the Stripe billing portal where you can:

- View your current plan and billing interval
- Update your payment method
- View invoices and payment history
- Cancel your subscription

## Switching billing intervals

During your trial, you can switch between monthly ($15/mo) and annual ($129/yr) billing directly in the app.

After your trial, use the Stripe billing portal to change your billing interval.

## Viewing your plan status

Your billing settings page shows:
- Current plan (Free or Pro)
- Billing interval (monthly or annual)
- Next renewal date
- Trial status and countdown (if applicable)
- Cancellation status (if cancellation is in progress)
