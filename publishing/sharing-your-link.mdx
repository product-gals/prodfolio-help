Your product manager portfolio is live — now let's get it in front of the right people. 🚀

## Your portfolio URL

Every published portfolio gets a public link:

```
prodfolio.io/p/your-slug
```

Pro users with a custom domain will also have their portfolio available at their custom URL.

## Copying your link

From the portfolio dashboard, click the **Copy Link** button on your portfolio card. The URL is copied to your clipboard.

## Where to share your PM portfolio

- **LinkedIn** — Add it to your profile's "Featured" section or share as a post (this is huge for visibility!)
- **Resume** — Include the link in your resume header
- **Job applications** — Paste it in the "Portfolio" or "Website" field
- **Email signature** — Add it as a clickable link
- **Twitter/X** — Share it with your network

<Tip>Pro tip: Adding your product manager portfolio to LinkedIn's Featured section gives it visibility every time someone views your profile. 💡</Tip>
