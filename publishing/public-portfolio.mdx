---
title: How Your Public Portfolio Looks
---

When someone visits your portfolio link, they see a professional, themed page showcasing your product management experience. It's organized into clean, scannable sections that are optimized for hiring managers to find what they are looking for. Here's what to expect. 👀

## What visitors see

- Your **name, title, and location** at the top
- **Headshot** (if uploaded)
- **About video** (if added, ✨ Pro)
- **Professional summary** and expertise tags
- **Availability status**
- **Work experience** timeline
- **Case studies** (clickable to read full SIGNAL writeups) with skills and tools tags
- **References** with quotes
- **Beyond Work** links

## SEO and sharing

Your product manager portfolio includes dynamic metadata for search engines and social media previews:
- Page title: Your name + title
- Description: Your professional summary
- Open Graph tags for rich link previews on LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.

This means when you share your PM portfolio link, it looks great everywhere — with a proper preview card, not just a plain URL. 🔗

<Tip>Recommended: if you're going to post on LinkedIn, try viewing what your post could look like with the [LinkedIn Post Inspector](https://www.linkedin.com/post-inspector/).</Tip>

## Theme

Your selected theme is applied to the entire page. Visitors see the colors and styling you've chosen.
