# How Your Public Portfolio Looks

When someone visits your portfolio link, they see a polished, themed page with your information organized into sections.

## What visitors see

- Your **name, title, and location** at the top
- **Headshot** (if uploaded)
- **About video** (if added, Pro only)
- **Professional summary** and expertise tags
- **Work experience** timeline
- **Case studies** (clickable to read full SIGNAL writeups)
- **References** with quotes
- **Beyond Work** links
- **Skills and tools** tags
- **Availability status**

## SEO and sharing

Your portfolio includes dynamic metadata for search engines and social media previews:
- Page title: Your name + title
- Description: Your professional summary
- Open Graph tags for rich link previews on LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.

## Inline editing for portfolio owners

When you visit your own published portfolio while logged in, you can edit section titles and subtitles directly on the page — no need to go back to the builder.

Hover over any section title or subtitle to see the edit pencil icon. Changes save automatically.

[Learn more about customizing section titles and subtitles →](/customizing/section-titles-subtitles)

## Theme

Your selected theme is applied to the entire page. Visitors see the colors and styling you've chosen.
