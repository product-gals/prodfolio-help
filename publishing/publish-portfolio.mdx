You've built your product manager portfolio — now it's time to share it with the world. 🌎

## Draft vs. Live

- **Draft** — Your portfolio is only visible to you. You can edit freely without visitors seeing changes.
- **Live** — Your portfolio is publicly accessible at your Prodfolio URL.

## How to publish

1. Open your portfolio from the dashboard
2. Click the **Publish** button
3. Your product manager portfolio is now live at `prodfolio.io/p/your-slug`

## Unpublishing

You can unpublish at any time by toggling the visibility off. This makes your portfolio inaccessible to visitors, but all your data is preserved.

<Tip>You don't need to fill in every section before publishing. Publish early, share with a few trusted contacts for feedback, then iterate. Your PM portfolio is a living document! 💜</Tip>

## First publish celebration

When you publish your portfolio for the first time, you'll see a celebration modal. You earned it! 🎉
