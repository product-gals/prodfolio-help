---
title: Publishing Your Portfolio
---

You've built your product manager portfolio, now it's time to share it with the world. 🌎

## Draft vs. Live

- **Draft**: Your portfolio is only visible to you. You can edit freely without visitors seeing changes.
- **Live**: Your portfolio is publicly accessible at your Prodfolio URL.

## How to publish

1. Open your portfolio from the dashboard
2. Click the **Publish** button
3. Your product manager portfolio is now live at `app.prodfolio.io/p/your-slug`

<Tip>If you're in the builder, you can click **Go Live** instead.</Tip>

## Unpublishing

You can unpublish at any time by toggling the visibility off. This makes your portfolio inaccessible to visitors, but all your data is preserved. You can find this in the **Domain** section of the builder.

<Tip>You don't need to fill in every section before publishing. Publish early, share with a few trusted contacts for feedback, then iterate. Your portfolio is a **living document**! 💜</Tip>

## First publish celebration

When you publish your portfolio for the first time, you'll see a celebration modal. You earned it! 🎉
