# Traffic Sources

<Note>Detailed traffic source data is a Pro feature. Free users see total view count only.</Note>

Prodfolio categorizes your visitors by how they found your portfolio.

## Source categories

- **Direct** — Typed your URL directly or used a bookmark
- **Organic** — Found you through a search engine
- **Referral** — Clicked a link from another website
- **Social Media** — Came from LinkedIn, Twitter, or other social platforms
- **Other** — Sources that don't fit the above categories

## How it works

Prodfolio analyzes the referrer data from each visit and categorizes it automatically. Bot traffic is filtered out so you only see real human visitors.

## Tips

- If most traffic is "Direct," your link-sharing strategy is working
- "Social Media" traffic spikes often correlate with posts you've shared
- Use traffic source data to double down on what's working
