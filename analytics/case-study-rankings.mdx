See which of your product case studies are getting the most attention. This is gold for understanding what resonates with hiring managers and visitors. ⭐

<Note>Case study rankings are a Pro feature. [Upgrade to Pro →](/billing/plan-comparison)</Note>

## What you'll see

- A ranked list of your case studies ordered by total views
- View counts for each case study
- Click-through rates from your portfolio to each case study

## How to use this data

- **Top-performing case studies**: Consider featuring these more prominently or sharing them directly
- **Low-performing case studies**: Review the titles and descriptions, they may need more compelling hooks
- **New case studies**: Give them time to accumulate views before drawing conclusions
