Prodfolio is a portfolio builder designed specifically for product managers. It gives you a professional, shareable portfolio to showcase your experience, case studies, and skills — without needing to build a personal website from scratch.

## Who is Prodfolio for?

- **Aspiring PMs** looking to break into product management
- **Job-seeking PMs** who want to stand out in applications
- **Mid-to-senior PMs** building their professional brand
- **PM leaders** showcasing their track record

## What can you do with Prodfolio?

- Build a polished portfolio with your experience, skills, and tools
- Write detailed case studies using the **SIGNAL framework**
- Upload and parse your resume with AI to auto-populate your portfolio
- Share a public link (e.g., `prodfolio.io/p/your-name`) or connect a custom domain
- Track who's viewing your portfolio with built-in analytics
- Password-protect sensitive case studies
