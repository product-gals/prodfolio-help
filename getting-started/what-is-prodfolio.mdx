title: What is Prodfolio?
---

Prodfolio is a portfolio builder made specifically for product managers, by product managers. 💜 In under an hour you can have a professional, shareable product management portfolio to share (without needing to build a website from scratch).

Think of it as your career's highlight reel. Whether you're prepping for interviews, building your personal brand, or just want a place to show off the work you're proud of - we've got you covered.

## Who is Prodfolio for?

Prodfolio is built for PMs at every stage of the product management journey:

- **Aspiring PMs** looking to break into product management and land their first PM role
- **Job-seeking PMs** who want their product manager portfolio to stand out in applications
- **Mid-to-senior PMs** building their professional brand and showcasing product leadership
- **Product leaders** documenting their track record of shipping great products

No matter where you are in your career, a strong product portfolio helps you tell your story. 🚀

## What can you do with Prodfolio?

- Build a shareable product manager portfolio with your experience, references, and side projects
- Write detailed product case studies using our **SIGNAL framework**
- Upload and parse your resume with AI to auto-populate your portfolio sections
- Share a public link (e.g., `app.prodfolio.io/p/your-name`) or connect a custom domain
- Track who's viewing your portfolio with built-in analytics
- Password-protect sensitive case studies for NDA-covered work
