# Creating Your Account

## Sign up with email

1. Go to [app.prodfolio.io/sign-up](https://app.prodfolio.io/sign-up)
2. Enter your first name, email address, and a password
3. Click **Sign Up**
4. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to confirm

<Note>You must verify your email before you can access the dashboard.</Note>

## Sign up with Google

1. Click **Continue with Google** on the sign-up page
2. Select your Google account
3. Complete your profile (name and professional title) on the next screen

## Password requirements

Your password must include:
- At least 8 characters
- One uppercase letter
- One lowercase letter
- One number
- One special character

## Next steps

After verifying your email, you'll be guided through the [onboarding flow](/getting-started/onboarding-walkthrough) to set up your profile and create your first portfolio.
