When you first sign in, Prodfolio walks you through a quick two-step onboarding to personalize your experience and set up your profile.

## Step 1: Tell us about yourself

You'll answer three quick questions:

- **Career stage** — Where are you in your PM journey? (Aspiring, Starting Out, Early Career, Mid-Career, Senior/Leadership)
- **Primary goal** — What are you hoping to accomplish? (Land first PM role, Job searching, Get promoted, Grow your presence, Just exploring)
- **How you heard about Prodfolio** — Helps us understand where our community comes from

## Step 2: Set up your profile

This is where you create your public identity:

- **First name and last name** (required)
- **Professional title** (required) — e.g., "Senior Product Manager"
- **LinkedIn URL** (optional)
- **Your portfolio slug** — This becomes your public URL: `prodfolio.io/p/your-slug`

<Tip>Choose a slug that's easy to remember — your name or a professional handle works best. You can check availability in real time.</Tip>

## What happens next

After completing onboarding, Prodfolio automatically creates your first portfolio. You'll land on the portfolio builder where you can start adding your experience, case studies, and more.
