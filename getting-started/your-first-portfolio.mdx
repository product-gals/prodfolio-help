# Your First Portfolio

This guide walks you through building and publishing your first Prodfolio portfolio from start to finish.

{/* TODO: Add video embed */}

## 1. Complete onboarding

After signing up and verifying your email, complete the [onboarding flow](/getting-started/onboarding-walkthrough). This creates your first portfolio automatically.

## 2. Fill in your basics

Start with the essentials in the **Basics** section:
- Your name, title, and location
- LinkedIn and GitHub URLs
- Contact email
- Availability status (Available, Open to Opportunities, Not Looking)

[Learn more about portfolio basics →](/building/portfolio-basics)

## 3. Write your About section

Add your professional summary, select your areas of expertise, and write your product philosophy.

[Learn more about the About section →](/building/about-section)

## 4. Add your experience

Add your work history with company names, titles, and dates. Toggle on descriptions to add detail about each role.

[Learn more about work experience →](/building/work-experience)

## 5. Create a case study

This is where your portfolio really shines. Use the SIGNAL framework to walk through a product story step by step.

[Learn more about case studies →](/building/case-studies)

## 6. Choose a theme

Pick from 5 preset themes to match your personal brand. Pro users can customize colors with hex codes.

[Learn more about themes →](/customizing/themes)

## 7. Publish

When you're ready, hit **Publish** to make your portfolio live. You'll get a shareable link at `prodfolio.io/p/your-slug`.

[Learn more about publishing →](/publishing/publish-portfolio)

<Tip>You don't need to fill in every section before publishing. Start with the basics and a case study, then build from there.</Tip>
